Overview

MultiStop Router ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

  • Location data (optional): Used to set your current location as a starting point on the map. This is processed locally and not shared with us.
  • Contacts data (optional): If you import contacts or addresses, they are stored only on your device. We do not collect or transmit your contacts.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated information such as app crashes or performance data through Google Play services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide routing and map functionality.
  • To allow importing/exporting your stops and contacts.
  • To improve app performance and reliability.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal information.
  • Ads in the Free version are served by Google AdMob, which may use cookies and device identifiers to provide personalized ads.
  • Purchases and subscriptions are handled through Google Play Billing. We do not process your payment details directly.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You may choose not to grant contacts access (manual entry is always available).
  • Pro users can upgrade to remove ads.

Data Storage & Security

  • All stops, routes, and contacts you save remain locally on your device.
  • We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but please use your device responsibly.
